Broccoli and Meatball Quinoa Bowl

  • Ingredients
  • 1/2 tsp

    garlic powder

  • 1/2 c.

    white quinoa

  • 1 c.

    chicken broth

  • 2 tsp.

    avocado oil

  • 1

    clove garlic

  • 1 c.

    broccoli

  • 1/2 lbs.

    ground chicken

  • 1 tsp.

    Italian seasoning

  • sea salt & black pepper to taste

  • 35min
  • 2 servings
  • 100 cal/serving
Direction

Preheat oven to 400ÂºF and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

Combine ground chicken, Italian seasoning, and garlic powder in a bowl. Use hands to mix well and form 8 meatballs. Arrange on baking sheet, and bake for 15-20 minutes, until cooked through.

Meanwhile, combine quinoa and chicken broth in a saucepan. Cover, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium low, and simmer for 10 minutes, until all liquid is absorbed.

While the quinoa is cooking, heat avocado oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and broccoli florets. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally. Season with sea salt and pepper.

Divide the quinoa between two bowls (or one bowl and one glass container, for leftovers). Top each bowl with 4 meatballs and half of the broccoli.

Easy Tomato, Cucumber and Red Onion Salad

  • Ingredients
  • 10

    Plum tomatoes

  • 1

    medium cucumber, halved

  • 1/2

    red onion

  • 2

    garlic cloves

  • 1

    clove garlic

  • 2

    Tablespoons good-quality olive oil

  • 2

    Tablespoons red wine vinegar

  • 2 tsp.

    chopped fresh parsley

  • Coarse salt and black pepper

  • 10min
  • 2 servings
  • 30 cal/serving
Direction

In a large bowl, combine tomatoes, cucumber, onion, garlic, olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, pepper and parsley. Let stand at room ADVERTISEMENT temperature to allow flavors to marinate, at least 30 minutes and up to 4 hours. Toss with a large spoon, sprinkle with feta and serve.

Grilled Chicken

  • Ingredients
  • 1

    skinless chicken breast fillet

  • 150gr

    cherry tomatoes

  • 28gr

    fresh basil

  • 70gr

    wild rocket leaves

  • 3 tsp.

    vegetable oil

  • 1 c.

    broccoli

  • 1

    red onion

  • 1

    lime

  • 15min
  • 2 servings
  • 100 cal/serving
Direction

Preheat the grill to high. Drizzle the chicken with 1 tbsp oil, season with salt and pepper and grill for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.

While the chicken is cooking, prepare a vinaigrette - mix the vinegar and 2 tbsp oil in a bowl, season with salt and pepper and set aside.

Toss the vinaigrette with the rocket, cherry tomatoes and basil. Divide the salad between plates, top with the grilled chicken and serve immediately.
